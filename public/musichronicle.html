Musichronicle

My intentions with this small project is to document the music that I've recently come to adore, and to share these beautiful sounds with the rest of you!

I usually have trouble recalling the music I'm fond of, and the artists that express with sounds, what I couldn't with words. I hope this project succeeds at keeping track of these very artists, and I look forward to continuously updating this page as time goes on.

2022

Son Lux - Tomorrows   experimental

I was recently reintroduced to Son Lux, a band of three extremely talented individuals, by a couple of relatives who went to see their concert. Although I didn't attend, I'm glad to have been reminded of Son Lux – it had been four whole years since I last listened to their works.

This album is no different, every single track is simply terrific.

The element that makes Son Lux stand out the most in this particular genre, is the distinctiveness of their sound, which can be considered as having been meticulously engineered to serve the purpose of exposing you to something you never imagined could exist, all the while conforming to a rather sophisticated musical structure.
